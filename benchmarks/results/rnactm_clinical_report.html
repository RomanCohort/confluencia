Model Description

RNACTM Six-Compartment Model

The RNACTM model simulates circRNA pharmacokinetics through six compartments:

  1. Injection: Initial dose pool at injection site
  2. LNP: Lipid nanoparticle encapsulated circRNA
  3. Endosome: Intracellular endosomal compartment
  4. Cytoplasm: Cytoplasmic circRNA available for translation
  5. Protein: Translated therapeutic protein
  6. Clearance: Cumulative clearance (RNA + protein degradation)

Limitations and Assumptions

  1. Literature-derived parameters: RNACTM parameters are initialized from literature values rather than directly fitted to circRNA PK data.
  2. Limited external validation: Model validation is based on synthetic data and published literature values. Prospective validation against in vivo circRNA PK studies is required.
  3. Species extrapolation: Most parameter estimates are from mouse studies. Extrapolation to human requires additional allometric scaling and validation.
  4. Simplified compartment structure: The six-compartment model is a simplification. More complex models may be needed for specific applications.
